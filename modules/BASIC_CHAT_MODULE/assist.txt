<header>::::: Creating an assist macro :::::<end>

<highlight>Set a new assist<end>
<tab><symbol>assist 'name1' 'name2' 

<highlight>Show current assist<end>
<tab><symbol>assist

<highlight>Add player(s) to assist<end>
<tab><symbol>addassist 'name1' 'name2'

<highlight>Remove player from assist<end>
<tab><symbol>remassist 'name'

<highlight>Clear assist<end>
<tab><symbol>remassist

<highlight>Display Team Assist<end>
<tab><symbol>teamassist

<highlight>Set Team Assist<end>
<tab><symbol>teamassist 'team1' 'team2' 'team3'

<highlight>Clear Team Assist<end>
<tab><symbol>teamassist clear
