<header>::::: Scout Commands :::::<end>

<highlight>To scout a base:<end>
<tab><symbol>scout zone # close ct_ql side org
(all required info is written on Control Tower;
close time is 'created' time)
 
For instance:
<tab><symbol>scout PW 4 12:34:56 190 Omni Amphiaraus

<highlight>To scout a base bypassing closing time and org checks:<end>
<tab><symbol>forcescout zone # close ct side org

<highlight>To see the list of unscouted bases:<end>
<tab><symbol>scoutneeded

<highlight>Display top scouts<end>
<tab><symbol>topscout

<highlight>Unscout tower site (if incorrectly scouted)
<tab><symbol>unscout zone #
<tab><symbol>unscout PM 6
