<header>::::: Open/Openomni Commands :::::<end>
(openomni has exactly same syntax but only available for listed RLs)

<highlight>Open bases in 200-300 range<end>
<tab><symbol>open

<highlight>Open bases with particular CT ql<end>
<tab><symbol>open 'ct_ql'
<green><tab><symbol>open 100<end>

<highlight>Open bases with CT in range from X to Y<end>
<tab><symbol>open 'X' 'Y'
<green><tab><symbol>open 100 200<end>

<highlight>Open bases in particular zone<end>
<tab><symbol>open 'zone'
<green><tab><symbol>open EFP<end>
