<header>::::: Clan twinks list Commands :::::<end>

<highlight>Shows clans online<end>
<tab><symbol>clans

<highlight>Shows all clans in list<end>
<tab><symbol>clans all

<highlight>Adding clans to list<end>
<highlight>(only add people to this list if you seen them at tower wars)<end>
<tab><symbol>clans add 'name'

<highlight>Adding clans with known main<end>
<tab><symbol>clans add 'name' 'main'

<highlight>Adding main to 'name'<end>
<tab><symbol>clans add 'name' 'main'

<highlight>Remove clans from the list<end>
<tab><symbol>clans rem 'name'
